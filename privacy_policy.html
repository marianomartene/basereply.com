Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

BaseReply (“we,” “our,” or “us”) provides a hosted chat platform that allows businesses to manage conversations across multiple channels, including WhatsApp, Instagram, and website chat widgets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal data when you use our website and services.

1. Who We Are

BaseReply is operated by Mariano Martene, based in Portugal. We provide hosted installations of open-source software (Chatwoot, MIT License) with custom configuration and support for client businesses.

For any privacy-related inquiries, you can contact us at: privacy@basereply.com

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email, billing information, and company details.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, and device information.
  • Conversation Data: Messages and metadata from integrated channels.
  • Support Data: Communications with our support team.

We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 16 years of age.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to provide and improve our platform, enable integrations, communicate with you, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell or rent personal data.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process data based on contractual necessity, legitimate interest, and consent, where applicable.

5. Role of the Customer (Data Controller)

Customers act as Data Controllers, and BaseReply acts as a Data Processor, following their instructions when managing end-user data.

6. Data Sharing and Sub-Processors

We share limited data with trusted providers (e.g., DigitalOcean, Hetzner, Meta) under GDPR-compliant agreements.

7. International Data Transfers

Transfers outside the EEA are safeguarded by Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent protections.

8. Data Retention

Data is retained as long as necessary for service provision and legal obligations. You can request deletion anytime via email.

9. Security

We use encryption, access control, and secure hosting to protect data. No online system is completely secure.

10. Your Rights (EU Users)

You may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, or portability of your data. Contact us to exercise your rights.

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential and analytical cookies. You can disable them in your browser settings.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of our site constitutes acceptance of the latest version.

13. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to: privacy@basereply.com
